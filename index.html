Simon Memory Game

Put your memory to the test!

Instructions:

  1. Press any key to start/restart the game.
  2. One of the squares will then flash, you must click on this square.
  3. A second square will flash. Now you must click on the first square and then the second square.
  4. A third square will flash. You must click the first, second and third square in the correct order.
  5. The game will continue adding squares and you must try to repeat the entire sequence to get to the next level.
    6. Remember, if the screen goes red and makes the "RAZZ" sound it's game over and time to start again.
